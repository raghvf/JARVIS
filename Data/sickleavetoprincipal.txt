Here's a sample letter:

[Your Name]
[Your Class/Section]
[Your School/College Name]
[Date]

Respected Principal Sir/Ma'am,

I am writing to inform you that I was unable to attend school/college on [Date of Absence] due to health reasons. I was suffering from [Mention the illness, e.g. fever, cold, etc.] and was advised by my doctor to take rest and avoid any strenuous activities.

I am now feeling better and am expected to make a full recovery soon. I apologize for any inconvenience my absence may have caused and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as possible.

I have attached a medical certificate from my doctor, which confirms my illness and the dates of my absence. I request you to kindly grant me sick leave for the above-mentioned date.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

[Attach medical certificate, if required]

Note: You can modify the letter as per your needs and requirements. Make sure to include the following details:

* Date of absence
* Reason for absence (illness)
* Medical certificate (if required)
* Apology for any inconvenience caused
* Commitment to catch up on missed work
