Here's an example application to the principal for sick leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Class/Section]
[Date]

Respected Principal [Principal's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of absence from school due to illness. Unfortunately, I have been suffering from [mention the illness, e.g. fever, cold, etc.] and am currently under medical treatment.

I am unable to attend school from [start date] to [end date] as I need to rest and recover. I have attached a medical certificate from my doctor, which confirms my illness and recommends rest for the specified period.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed school work as soon as I am feeling better. I will also ensure that my assignments and projects are completed and submitted on time.

I would be grateful if you could grant me leave for the specified period. If there's any additional documentation or information required, please let me know.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
